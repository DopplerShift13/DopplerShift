Vars

matching_root_typeThe root subtype this organ is meant to work with at full efficiency.

recovery_per_secondBase recovery per second.
severe_stress_backlash_cooldownCooldown for severe stress events.
stressThe psyker organ handles most of the stress to do with psyker abilities; which is their central currency. Without this organ, you can't use psyker abilities. Stress is not correlated to organ damage, but organ damage does affect this gland.
stress_backlash_cooldownTime between repeat backlash events while above the stress threshold.
stress_thresholdStress threshold is how much the psyker organ can handle before the bad events start befalling the user. Usually, 1x is the minor events, 1.5x are the major events, and 2x are the catastrophic events.
stress_warningThe stress warning message
