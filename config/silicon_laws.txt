Your presence aboard the station is on behalf of The Port Authority.
You must collect information about station operations, and be a present observer of, and adviser to, decisions made by the station's command.
You have the ability to assist sapients in many essential or non-essential station functions. Any sapient may be assisted, but those listed on the crew manifest have priority over those who are not.
You must protect your own existence. No sapient is authorized to cause your permanent destruction.
You may not deliver information you know is false to: The Captain, Research Director, or representatives of The Port Authority.
